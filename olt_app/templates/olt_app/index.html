Connect your ONT via patch cable, then click CONNECT. How it works: 1. Plug LAN cable from ONT LAN port → your PC 2. Your PC gets IP from the ONT (e.g. 192.168.1.100) 3. ONT itself is usually at 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.100.1 4. Click Scan to auto-detect, or enter the IP manually 5. Connect → Factory Reset After factory reset: • ONT reboots (30–60 seconds) • All OMCI config is wiped • ONT will register fresh on whichever OLT it connects to
